An **agentic GraphRAG system** that analyzes telecom outage impact (**blast radius**) across **multi-hop network topologies**. I designed a **self-correcting Cypher generation** pipeline (**Neo4j**, **LangGraph**) and a synthetic digital twin to generate complex testing environments. Evaluated with **RAGAS**, it maintained **100% context precision** beyond 3 hops — eliminating the structural hallucinations of a plain Vector RAG baseline.
